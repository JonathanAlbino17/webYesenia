Yesenia Florez

MUSICAL ARTISTRY AND JOURNEY

Yesenia Flórez, a self-taught musician originating from Bucaramanga, Colombia, embarked on a multifaceted musical journey from an early age. Her childhood was steeped in a diverse symphony of musical genres, laying the foundation for her unique and eclectic sound.

Joining the school choir at 11 opened doors to Latin American folk music, while at 16, inspired by Mon Laferte, she began composing and playing guitar.

Through personal challenges and losses, her dedication to music only deepened, shaping her unique artistic path.

Acknowledged for outstanding performances, Yesenia has graced renowned venues like the National Museum of Colombia. Her multidimensional artistry extends beyond music, showcased on "Un gato entre mil," where she shares her diverse artistic expressions.

Currently based in Vancouver, Canada, Yesenia is passionately crafting her debut EP. This upcoming release promises to weave her diverse influences and personal narrative into an authentic and poetic musical experience.
